About

David Wilson-Brown is a student of web development, educator, actor, and director. His approach and work in web development ties directly to his creative process in stage and film. The end result of a website or app, and a film or play, is ultimately a functional product that successfully fills a need for the user (audience). They are both born as an idea, and through extensive analysis, testing (rehearsal) and collaboration, are created in hopes to improve the quality of life.

Along with training in web development from Austin Coding Academy, David holds a Master of Fine Arts in Performance from the University of Wisconsin-Madion and a BFA in Theatre Performance from UT Arlington.
